On this site we will be exploring the various different military aircraft used across the globe. There are many types of military aircraft but i will mainly be focusing on jet powered aircraft

The above image is a F-22 "Raptor"

The F-22 "raptor" is a jet engine powered fighter class aircraft specialized in air to air combat, it features advanced stealth capabilities to infiltrate enemy territory without being detected by enemy radar.


In the air-to-air configuration the Raptor carries six AIM-120 AMRAAMs and two AIM-9 Sidewinders

These are one of the various configurations for the F-22 "Raptor", this specific aircraft is extremely expensive and as such America did not produce too many, their main role in military operations is to acheive air superiority by downing enemy aircraft.
